Wanda Harland

Previous Page

My first project in year 13 DDT was to design a real life store's window display in a group of three using a crate and piece of acrylic. We had to approach our chosen business, which we decided to be the store 'Wanda Harland' which is in Petone, Wellington. The process of this involved making a display that included the stores current aesthetic however, with a completely new design and a new logo. The stickers where designed using Adobe Illustrator, printed using a vinyl cutter and then placed on the acrylic glass. We then used a 3D printer to create the block letters displaying the words 'Wanda Harland'. For the wallpaper, we used paper out of a maths book and then cut these into geometrical shapes to match the vinyl stickers.
